## Highlights

- At Truffle's peak there were almost 100 boxes with adoption by many Fortune 50 companies including AirBus, Starbucks, and EY.

## Overview

Truffle Boxes were my entry into web3 and the reason I got hired in the first place. I built them in public and published them on Reddit (you can see the original website above). The Founder and CEO of Truffle reached out to me via that Reddit post and offered me an interview. From there I worked at Truffle for almost 5 years as employee #1, eventually serving as Lead Product Manager.

Truffle Boxes are boilerplates to quickly get started developing smart contracts and dapps with Truffle. Many large companies have made or used Truffle Boxes including: Airbus, EY, Microsoft, and Starbucks.
