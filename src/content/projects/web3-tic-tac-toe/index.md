## Highlights

- Featured in the Unity Asset Store as a web3 game development example.
- Fully functional game with wallet integration, betting, and payout system.

## Overview

Consensys needed to demonstrate an integration between their wallet (MetaMask), developer tools (Truffle Suite), and Unity as part of our partnership with Unity around web3 gaming. I put together an example Tic Tac Toe game that would allow two players to connect their wallest and place a bet on who would win. The bets were place in an escrow contract and winner could withdraw once the game was complete.
